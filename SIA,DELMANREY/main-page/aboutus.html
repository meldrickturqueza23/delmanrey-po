Honorato Guzman Baquiran (HGB) College

is a private educational institution in Tumauini, Isabela. Since its foundation is 2005, the school provides qualitiy education that builds graduates who are competent, active, and adaptive in navigating problems in both the local and global communities.


HG Baquiran College

is recognized by Commision on Higher Education (CHED) and the Department of Education in Teacher Education, Business Administration, Criminology, Information Technology, and Hotel and Restaurant Management.
